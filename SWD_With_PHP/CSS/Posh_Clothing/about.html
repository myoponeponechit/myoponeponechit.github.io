Posh Clothing

Founded in 2010,Posh clothing is famous for its opulent and unique style.It is an upmarket and innovative designer founded by Mrs.Chue and based in Yangon. It also runs its own world-renowned designer school.

Why the page works: The primary function of an About page is to get the point of your business across to the reader quickly. Posh clothing achieves this with the founder’s initial and guiding goal presented in the first paragraph in the form of a quotation. Within 6 to 8 seconds, you’re aware of the shop’s customer feedback and what you’ll get from this shop.

What else works?: Mrs.Chue goes on to mention clothes orders from faraway place such as Bangkok. Subtly weaving in achievements and social proof of your success is an incredibly powerful element for any About Us page.

Design and format: page design is refreshingly simple and is complimented with a beautiful and welcoming illustration of their shop front. Social media buttons and menu options are all clearly visible.
